About Us

Spotware Systems is a financial technology provider, developing and offering its own proprietary solutions, developed in-house at the headquarters in Limassol, Cyprus. Spotware’s product line is geared towards online trading providers in the eFX, Cryptocurrencies and multi-asset CFD space.
